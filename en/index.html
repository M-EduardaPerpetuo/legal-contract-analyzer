    Welcome to the Legal Contract Analyzer

    The Legal Contract Analyzer is an enterprise-grade automated solution for legal risk screening and analysis. Leveraging state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence, the system acts as a high-precision initial filter for legal departments and law firms.

    The Problem We Solve

    Manual review of service agreements, NDAs, and corporate contracts is a slow, repetitive process prone to human error. This system eliminates the bottleneck of initial screening, allowing legal professionals to focus solely on strategic decision-making.

    Key Benefits

    • Efficiency: Drastic reduction in reading time and identification of critical clauses.
    • File Flexibility: Native support for text extraction across .pdf, .docx, and .xlsx formats.
    • Legal Security: Proactive identification of hidden liabilities using the Gemini 2.5 Flash model.
    • Human-in-the-Loop: The AI does not replace the lawyer; it issues a pre-formatted executive summary that strictly requires final human approval.
